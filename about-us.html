The Story of Tech Serve4 U

Our story starts with the realistic thought of readiness - from a rookie to a pro, a bookish to on and off-field expert.


We have observed versatile individuals failing in their lives. They wanted to win but didn’t know how to do that. Slowly, they lost themselves in the crowd.


Our pathfinder and leader, Mr. Shiblu Ahmad, is a long-term experienced engineer. He was once an employee of another organization. Later, he had the vision to make the people dreaming, transforming the dream into reality, and give support all the time.


Mr. Ahmad has seen many people seeking a better place where they can learn and implement the ideas practically. As most of the training centers just “SELL” their courses to the students, he started to work differently and uniquely.


He was not up to showing unfeasible dreams, illusions. All the focuses were on “Workforce Readiness” instead of the so-called “Training.”


As a result, Tech Serve 4 U, LLC. was born!

  • The Beginning
    Tech Serve4 U has started its journey in 2013. It was potentially growing from the small team of 3-4 members. The team was enthusiastic about generating ideas on developing programs, software, and cloud computing systems to bring something newer.

    We have joined the Google Play Store, Appstore and added two efficient and productive applications like FindMe and Field Force. These apps have positive and satisfying user reviews. Users have found them beneficial in their real lives.

    FindMe is a free Real-time tracking application based on Android and iOS operating systems. It helps to share and track location instantly. It helps to connect with friends and family.


    Field Force is a useful app for managing the field employees’ activities together Online. This freebie offers more than typical management applications. It is absolutely free, unlike other apps which are paid versions.


    With the success and admirations of these smartphone applications, Tech Serve4 U later focused on cloud-based IT development services to follow Agile Scrum and microservice architecture.


    We are also working on building Next Generation Tracking Service for friends and family, intra-group or intra-organization tracking.
